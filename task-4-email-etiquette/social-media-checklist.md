# **SOCIAL MEDIA DO’S AND DON’TS**

**Do’s:**

1. Use passwords to keep your accounts safe and secure

2. Share useful and positive content

3. Keep your profiles professional

4. Be respectful of what others think online

5. Always check if information is true before you share it


**Don’ts:**

1. You should never share your things or sensitive information with anyone on the internet.

2. When you post something make sure the internet post is not bad or mean to other people.

3. Do not fight with people on the internet or social media or online communities. Internet fights and bullying are not good for the internet or the people who use the internet including media platforms and online communities.

4. Be careful when you see links on the internet. Do not click on internet links that look strange or bad.

5. The internet has a lot of information so do not share information about something, on the internet or social media platforms. Wrong information can cause a lot of trouble for you the people you know and the people who use media platforms and the internet.
