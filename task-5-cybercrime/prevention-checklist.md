*Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist*

* Never share your UPI PIN, OTP or passwords with anyone.

* Be careful with payment requests on UPI apps. Don't approve them if they seem suspicious.

* Always check who you are making payments to.

* Don't click on links from SMS, email or social media.

* Use strong passwords and unique for all your online accounts.

* Only use official and trusted apps for online transactions and downloads

* Be cautious of calls or messages from people who claim to be from banks or government.

* Don't share details like Aadhaar number or bank details , or password.

If you face cybercrime report it right away, on the National Cyber Crime Portal(https://cybercrime.gov.in) or call 1930.
